Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

foodnfitness.ai ("we", "us", "our") operates the wellness platform at foodnfitness.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following when you use the Service:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you sign up.
  • Profile information: optional details such as age, weight, biological sex, and food allergies you provide to personalise your results.
  • Daily check-in data: if you use the daily body score feature, we store your self-reported energy, sleep, and body feeling scores. This data is private to you only.
  • Usage data: your search queries and wellness responses, stored to power your conversation history.
  • Payment information: payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. We receive confirmation of successful transactions only.
  • Technical data: browser type, device, and anonymised usage analytics to improve the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise wellness recommendations.
  • To track your daily body score and surface patterns over time.
  • To manage your account and process payments via Stripe.
  • To improve the Service through anonymised analytics.
  • To communicate important updates about your account.

4. B2B Workplace Wellness

If you access foodnfitness.ai through a workplace invitation, your employer (the workspace admin) cannot see your individual searches, health data, daily scores, or any personal information. Employers only see anonymous, aggregated group-level trends — for example, the most common wellness topics searched across the team as a whole. Your personal wellness data is always private to you.

5. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase, with SOC 2 compliance, encryption at rest and in transit. Data is stored in EU or US servers depending on your region.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted third parties to operate the Service:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication.
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Anthropic — AI model powering wellness responses. Queries are not stored by Anthropic beyond the processing window.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@foodnfitness.ai.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@foodnfitness.ai
