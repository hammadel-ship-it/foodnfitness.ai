You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access until then. No partial refunds are issued for unused days in a billing period.

Corporate and team plans are governed by the terms of your signed agreement. The 14-day free pilot period allows your organisation to evaluate the Service fully before any payment is required. After the pilot, payments are non-refundable except as specified in your written agreement.

Exceptions

We may issue refunds at our sole discretion in cases of significant technical failure on our part that prevented you from using the Service entirely. To request an exception, contact us within 7 days of your purchase with details of the issue.
