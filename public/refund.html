Refund Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

We want you to be happy with foodnfitness.ai. If you are not satisfied, we will do our best to make it right.

Starter Pack (One-Time Purchase)

If you purchase the Starter pack and are unsatisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase, provided you have used fewer than 3 of your 10 credits.

To request a refund, email us at hello@foodnfitness.ai with your order details. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to your original payment method.

Monthly Subscriptions (Thrive & Optimise)

For monthly subscriptions:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access to your credits until then.
  • We do not offer partial refunds for unused credits within a billing period.
  • If you believe you were charged in error, contact us within 14 days and we will investigate and refund if appropriate.

Exceptional Circumstances

We review refund requests on a case-by-case basis. If you have experienced a technical issue that prevented you from using the Service, or if you were charged incorrectly, please contact us and we will resolve it promptly.

How to Request a Refund

Email hello@foodnfitness.ai with:

  • Your account email address.
  • Your order or transaction ID (found in your Paddle receipt email).
  • The reason for your refund request.

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

Contact

For any questions about this policy: hello@foodnfitness.ai
